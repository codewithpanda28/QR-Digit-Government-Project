# 🔒 Security & Privacy Update

## 1. Owner Verification 🛡️
- **Edit/Dashboard Access**: Now restricted to the **QR Owner** only.
- **Public View**: If someone else scans your QR code, they **cannot** see the "Edit" or "Dashboard" buttons. They only see the Safety Profile.

## 2. Phone Number Masking 🎭
- **Privacy**: Emergency contact numbers are now **masked** for public scanners (e.g., `+91 98******10`).
- **Functionality**: The "Call" button still works perfectly, connecting the call without revealing the full number text on screen.
- **Owner View**: The owner sees the full number.

## 3. Ownership Claiming 🤝
- When you activate a QR code, it is now securely linked to your account ID.
