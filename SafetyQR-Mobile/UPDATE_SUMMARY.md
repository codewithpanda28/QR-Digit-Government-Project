# 🛠️ Fixes & Context-Aware Updates

## 1. Context-Aware "Add More Information" 🧠
Now, the options you see when clicking "Add More Information" change based on the category you selected!

- **🚗 Vehicle Safety**: Shows "Insurance Policy", "Chassis Number", "Mechanic Contact".
- **👶 Child Safety**: Shows "School Name", "Roll Number", "Class/Section".
- **👵 Senior Safety**: Shows "Hospital Name", "Regular Medicine", "Caretaker".
- **🐾 Pet Safety**: Shows "Breed", "Vet Name", "Microchip ID".
- **🛡️ Women Safety**: Shows "Work Address", "Trusted Neighbor".

*(Common options like Blood Group, Doctor, and Custom Field are always available)*
